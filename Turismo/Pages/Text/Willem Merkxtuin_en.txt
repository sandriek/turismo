In the middle of the busy city centre of Breda, you will find a special and quiet spot. Behind the former Catharine street you'll find the Willem hofhuizen Mackay garden. The green oasis is named after William Mackay who was Mayor of Breda from 1967 to 1983. This beautiful garden was as it were a farewell gift for the Mayor of the inhabitants of Breda. In the garden are 14 sculptures. The Willem Mackay garden is open to visitors between 09.00 and 17.00, admission is free.
