The OAT market is a square in the city centre of Breda. It is near the Grote or Onze-Lieve-Vrouwekerk and the port. It is a small square with around cafés with terraces and a well-known entertainment place in Breda. Among other things, there is the monumental building the bird Saliba with asymmetrical stepped gable from 1665.

On the OAT market is the image the Troubadour. The Heron Street, just like the fish market Street, from on the OAT market. Via the havermarkt is 't Sas accessible.
