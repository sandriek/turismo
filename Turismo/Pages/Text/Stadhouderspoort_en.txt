The Stadhouderspoort is one of the gates of the castle of Breda in the Centre of Breda. The Castle has a moat, but the Stadhouderspoort can be reached over a walkway, after first the tap port with port building by to have gone. The Stadhouderspoort is equipped with a triangular pediment and bears the coat of arms of William V around which the motto of the order of the Garter "Honi soit qui mal y pense" (shame on him who thinks evil of). The South side of the port was restored in 1959.
