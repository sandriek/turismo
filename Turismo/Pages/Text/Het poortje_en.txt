In the karrestraat you can find lots of different stores, but also some points of interest. On the left you'll see a red gate. On the stone gate are sheep heads and the coat of arms of Breda. Behind the doors of the gate is a lane that leads to the back of the Grand place. Former use this gate if deterioration of de Vleeshal on the Grote Markt. The gate is made in 1614 by stonemason Melchior of Herbach. Until 1995 the Breda's Museum in this building was confirmed.
