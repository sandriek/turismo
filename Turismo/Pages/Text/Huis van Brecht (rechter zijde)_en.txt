Around the castle of Breda found you until the 16th century many beautiful houses. Here lived the Chief courtiers and guests were staying during their visit to the Court. By the city fire in 1534, many of these beautiful houses destroyed. One of the most beautiful and oldest hofhuizen of Breda, the House of Brecht, this city fire survived. The White House with the green gate is named after Jonker Godevaart by Brecht that the property. In the House have rich merchants and officers lived. There was a military hospital in established and after the second world war the House was part of the KMA. Since 1993, the library of the KMA in the House of Brecht.
